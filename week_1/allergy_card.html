Coeliac Disease Card

I have Coeliac Disease (Severe Gluten Allergy)

I cannot eat any foods containing gluten, even in very small amounts.
Please ensure my meal contains NO:

  • wheat
  • barley
  • rye
  • regular flour
  • breadcrumbs
  • soy sauce (unless gluten-free)

Important:

My food must also avoid cross-contamination.
Please prepare it using clean utensils, surfaces, and cooking oil that have not touched gluten.

Safe foods usually include:

  • plain meat or fish
  • vegetables
  • rice
  • potatoes
  • gluten-free products

Thank you for helping keep my meal safe.
